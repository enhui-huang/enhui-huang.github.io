LIX, École Polytechnique & Université Paris-Saclay --- Enhui Huang is a third-year Ph.D. student at [LIX](https://www.lix.polytechnique.fr/), [École polytechnique](https://www.polytechnique.edu/en) & [Université Paris-Saclay](https://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en), under the supervision of Prof. [Yanlei Diao](http://www.lix.polytechnique.fr/~yanlei.diao/) and Prof. [Anna Liu](http://people.math.umass.edu/~anna/). She is also a member of [CEDAR team](https://team.inria.fr/cedar/). She received her B.S.c degree in [Hongyi Mathematics Base Class](http://202.114.70.140/Englishversion/) from [Wuhan University](http://en.whu.edu.cn/), China in 2015 and her M.Sc. degree in Data Science from [École polytechnique](https://www.polytechnique.edu/en) & [Université Paris-Saclay](https://www.universite-paris-saclay.fr/en), France in 2016.
